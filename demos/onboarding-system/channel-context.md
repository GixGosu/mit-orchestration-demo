# Meridian Labs — Onboarding Knowledge Base

## Company Overview

**Mission:** Making AI accessible to every industry.

**Founding Story:** Meridian Labs was founded in 2019 by a group of AI researchers and industry veterans who saw a gap between cutting-edge machine learning research and real-world applications. Starting from a small office in Austin, TX, the team built tools that made it easy for non-technical teams to integrate AI into their workflows. Today, Meridian Labs has grown to roughly 200 employees and serves clients across healthcare, logistics, finance, and education.

### Core Values

1. **Build in the Open** — We default to transparency. Decisions, roadmaps, and even failures are shared openly so everyone can learn and contribute.
2. **Ship with Purpose** — Speed matters, but direction matters more. Every feature, fix, and experiment should tie back to real user impact.
3. **Lift as You Climb** — Individual success means nothing if the team falls behind. Mentor, document, and share knowledge relentlessly.
4. **Question the Defaults** — "We've always done it this way" is not a reason. Challenge assumptions, propose alternatives, and back them with data.
5. **Stay Human** — We build AI, but we are not machines. Rest, boundaries, empathy, and humor are non-negotiable parts of how we work.

### Org Structure

Meridian Labs is organized into the following departments:

- **Engineering** — Builds and maintains all products and infrastructure
- **Product** — Defines roadmap, prioritizes features, manages releases
- **Design** — Owns UX/UI, design system, and user research
- **Data Science** — Model training, evaluation, data pipelines, and ML research
- **Sales** — Business development, customer relationships, and revenue
- **HR** — People operations, recruiting, benefits, and culture
- **Finance** — Accounting, budgeting, payroll, and compliance

## Frequently Asked Questions

**1. Where do I park?**
Park in Garage B. Your badge grants access to the garage. If your badge doesn't work on Day 1, ask security at the front desk for a temporary pass.

**2. Is there a dress code?**
No formal dress code — we're casual. Wear whatever makes you comfortable. No policy, no judgment.

**3. What's the remote work policy?**
We follow a hybrid model: 3 days in-office (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday) and flexible on Monday and Friday. You can work from home on flex days or come in — your call.

**4. How do I submit expense reports?**
Use Expensify. Expenses under $100 are auto-approved. Expenses over $100 require manager approval. Submit within 30 days of the expense.

**5. How does the learning budget work?**
You get $2,000/year for professional development — books, online courses, conferences, workshops. Just get your manager's OK before purchasing. No formal approval form needed.

**6. When can I get a laptop refresh?**
Every 3 years. If your laptop has issues before then, contact Casey Morgan (@casey.m) in IT for repairs or a loaner.

**7. Is there relocation support?**
Yes — ask Robin Hayes (@robin.h) in HR for details. Packages vary by role and location.

**8. What's the referral bonus?**
$5,000 for each successful referral hire. Paid after the new hire completes 90 days. Submit referrals through the careers portal.

**9. When are performance reviews?**
Biannual: March and September. Your manager will share a self-review form 2 weeks before the review period.

**10. How does the promotion process work?**
Promotions are reviewed during performance cycles (March and September). Your manager nominates you based on sustained impact at the next level. Calibration happens at the department level.

**11. When do I join the on-call rotation?**
After 3 months. Your manager will onboard you to the on-call process, PagerDuty setup, and escalation procedures before your first shift.

**12. Are there meeting-free days?**
Meeting-free Fridays are encouraged (not mandatory). Try to keep Fridays clear for deep work, learning, or wrapping up the week.

**13. What are the lunch options?**
There's a cafeteria on the ground floor (subsidized, open 11:30–1:30). Food trucks visit on Tuesdays and Thursdays in the parking lot.

**14. How do I connect to guest wifi?**
Network name: "meridian-guest" — no password required. Use it for personal devices.

**15. Can I get company merch shipped to me?**
Yes! Post in #swag on Slack with your size and shipping address. The People team sends a welcome kit to all new hires.

**16. What mental health resources are available?**
Free Headspace subscription for all employees plus 6 EAP (Employee Assistance Program) sessions per year, fully covered. Contact HR for enrollment details.

**17. Are there commuter benefits?**
Yes — $150/month pre-tax commuter benefit for transit or parking. Enroll through the benefits portal.

**18. Is there a sabbatical program?**
Yes — 4 weeks paid sabbatical after 5 years of continuous employment. Coordinate timing with your manager and HR at least 3 months in advance.
