## :clipboard: Day 1 Checklist

Here's everything on your plate for Day 1. Take it step by step — no rush!

- [ ] **Laptop configuration** (45 min) — Pick up your laptop (Mac or Linux, your choice) from IT on the 2nd floor. Casey Morgan will walk you through initial setup.
- [ ] **Account provisioning** (30 min) — Set up your Slack, email (@meridian.dev), GitHub (meridian-labs org), and Figma accounts. IT will send credentials to your personal email.
- [ ] **VPN & 2FA setup** (20 min) — Install the VPN client (vpn.meridian.dev) and enroll in 2FA using 1Password. Follow the step-by-step guide IT provides.
- [ ] **Badge photo** (10 min) — Visit the front desk to take your badge photo. Your badge grants access to the building, garage B, and secure areas.
- [ ] **Meet your manager 1:1** (30 min) — A 30-minute introductory 1:1 with your direct manager. Expect to discuss team goals, your first-week plan, and any initial questions.
- [ ] **Office tour with buddy** (30 min) — Your assigned onboarding buddy will show you around: kitchen, restrooms, quiet rooms, phone booths, and the best coffee spots.

## :question: Top 5 FAQs

**1. Where do I park?**
Garage B — your badge grants access. If your badge doesn't work on Day 1, ask security at the front desk for a temporary pass.

**2. What's the remote work policy?**
Hybrid: 3 days in-office (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday), flexible on Monday and Friday. Work from home or come in — your call.

**3. How do I submit expense reports?**
Use Expensify. Under $100 = auto-approved. Over $100 = manager approval. Submit within 30 days.

**4. How does the learning budget work?**
$2,000/year for books, courses, conferences, and workshops. Just get your manager's OK before purchasing — no formal form needed.

**5. What mental health resources are available?**
Free Headspace subscription for all employees, plus 6 EAP (Employee Assistance Program) sessions per year, fully covered. Contact HR to enroll.
